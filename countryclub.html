Country Club


The Club offers a variety of options from short term to lifetime memberships. While the highlight of the Club must surely be the Jack Nicklaus designed championship course, members can also look forward to enjoying artificial clay surface tennis courts, international standard squash and badminton courts, bowling lanes with the latest AMF technology, indoor swimming and a state of-the-art gym.

 

Facilities and Sports Activities

Elegant furnishings. Beds fit for a king or queen, with comfort truly unmatched. Spacious marble clad bathrooms with walk-in showers and bathtubs to enjoy spectacular views. Superior Rooms are found in the Lagoon and Waterfall Wings, meanwhile Ocean Rooms are located at Seaview building.

Membership

Membership of the Country Club makes The Empire Hotel even more personal to you. A range of activities and facilities are at your disposal with which to entertain others or take time for yourself. Relax in style and enjoy golf, fitness, sports, swimming, spa and all recreation facilities as a member of The Empire Country Club.
